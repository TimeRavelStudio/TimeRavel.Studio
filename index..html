Accessible Digital Life
For Everyone

The TimeRavel.Studio is a start-up company making accessible games for everyone. We believe we should not talk about non-sighted, blind, partially sighted or sighted, deaf, hard of hearing or hearing (or any other) player. This list in itself is just a way too long. No. We should only talk about The Player. And everybody has to be included in.

We are focusing on developing accessible games for everyone. Our vision is to build up a world where we all can live together and enjoy it, and we have already started in our digital gaming universe and hope we can extend it together. We know that more and more of you are going to join the way we are already on with so many others. We are not pioneers. We are not doing anything new. We are just doing what we believe, everybody should be doing so.
